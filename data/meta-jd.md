# Agentic AI Engineer — Deccan AI

**Location:** Remote-first, team hubs in Bengaluru + SF.
**Level:** Senior individual contributor (staff-level trajectory).
**Team:** Joining a 6-person team building production agent systems for marketplaces and expert networks.

## The role

We're building agent infrastructure that matches expert talent to high-stakes work — interest modeling, simulated outreach, evidence-cited scoring. You'll own end-to-end systems: from prompt architecture and model routing to eval harnesses and the production agents themselves.

Concretely you will:

- Design and ship multi-agent pipelines (planner / reactor / verifier patterns) against frontier LLMs
- Build evaluation harnesses that catch regressions before they ship
- Run inference-optimization work: model selection per stage, context engineering, cost/latency tradeoffs
- Partner with product on rubric design and calibration
- Ship under real constraints (latency budgets, public deploys, observable systems)

## Must-haves

- 5+ years of software engineering with at least 2 years shipping production ML/AI systems
- Demonstrated work with modern LLM tooling: agent frameworks, eval harnesses, RAG pipelines, or tool-use patterns
- Production ownership of a non-trivial system from prompt design through deploy (portfolio, OSS, or prior role)
- Comfort with ambiguity — this role is heavier on judgment than on executing a spec

## Nice-to-haves

- Published writing on agent systems, evals, or prompt engineering
- Background with distributed systems at scale
- Experience with structured-output generation (JSON mode, grammars, constrained decoding)
- Familiarity with Vercel / Next.js / TypeScript for the product surfaces
- OSS contributions to agent frameworks (LangGraph, LlamaIndex, CrewAI, Pydantic AI, etc.)

## What 6 months in looks like

- You've shipped at least one production agent pipeline that we evaluate and trust
- You've raised the floor on our eval harness — we catch regressions we previously missed
- You're routinely the person who answers *"why is this model the right choice for this stage?"* — with data

## What this role is not

- Not a research role — we ship
- Not a people-management role
- Not a prototype factory — we care about production reliability, cost, and latency
